How interact-by dates work

Each entity's interact-by date is determined by two rules:

  • If the entity has never been interacted with, the interact-by date is one interval from the tracking start date.
  • Otherwise, the interact-by date is two intervals from the most recent interaction.

The default interval is 90 days, but you can set a custom interval per entity or change your account default in Settings. This approach works best as a guideline rather than a strict rule. It was inspired by The Minimalists' 90/90 Rule.
